Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2025

Thank you for using the PulzAI app (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Heart Rate Data: Heart rate data measured through the camera or synchronized from the Health app
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, etc.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you use the App, such as feature usage frequency, app crash information, etc.
  • Preference Settings: Your preferences set in the App, such as notification settings, measurement duration, etc.

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the App's functionality
  • Generating heart rate trend analysis and health advice
  • Sending heart rate abnormality alert notifications (if enabled)
  • Analyzing App usage to optimize user experience
  • Resolving technical issues and app crashes

3. Data Storage and Security

Your heart rate data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you choose to sync with the Health app, data will be processed according to Apple Health app's privacy policy.

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our or others' rights, property, or safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

You can exercise these rights through the App settings or by contacting us.

6. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated Privacy Policy will be posted in the App and marked with the effective date.
